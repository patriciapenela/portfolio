WE ARE JUST PIONS

This project came about after an internal reflection during the time our life stopped
because of the covid-19 virus.
I stopped, reflected, wrote, and photographed.

“We are just pawns in the hands of mother nature.
It was all so easy just a few months ago,
the touch, the care, the sincere smiles,
it was all so easy wasn't it?

We take for granted everything we have,
and look at us now.
What is left?

Even the very air we used to breathe
and took for granted has been taken away from us!

We live constantly thinking that we control everything around us
and in such a short time we have seen
that nothing we believed was that clear

We created a world in our illusion.
How naive and insignificant we are.
Us being just pawns in the hands of mother nature.”
Kisses.
