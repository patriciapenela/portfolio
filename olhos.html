WE ARE LIKE BOOKS

This picture came up after a moment of reflection on how the human being can be like a book.
Books teach us many things, tell us many stories, when we look inside a book we get to know a huge universe of interesting facts and stories.
If we think about it, all of us, human beings, are also like that, we all have stories to tell and we all have knowledge that we can share with others. Furthermore, we all have a cover, the cover that we show to the world, the way we present ourselves and make ourselves known.
It's funny isn't it? How we are so much like books after all.
