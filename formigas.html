AS FORMIGAS

This project was developed in the scope of the curricular unit of Editorial Design I of the 2nd year of the degree. To elaborate it, we were given this book and asked to make a cover for it. In the accomplishment of this project, besides the contents mentioned above, I also took the initiative to make a bookmark so that the reader would have something to mark the page where he/she is so that he/she doesn't bend the corners of the book as many readers do, and in this way the book is much more preserved.
